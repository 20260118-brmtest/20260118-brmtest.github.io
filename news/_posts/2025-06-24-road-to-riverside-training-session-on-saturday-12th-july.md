Planning on heading to our Riverside Riichi tournament in October? Or just looking to up your game? This event is for you!

---

- Where: Bristol Bridge Club
- When: Exact times TBC but likely 10am-6pm
- Who: All players of all abilities welcome, so long as you can play unaided
- Cost: free (lunch not provided)
- **Signup required, attendees accepted in groups of 8 only** - form below

---

For this meetup, we will form groups of 8 players. 4 of these will play a hand as part of a hanchan. The others become watchers and each watch one player, making notes if need be. After each hand, everyone will review and discuss any interesting decision points and potential lessons.
