We held our first ever Annual General Meeting! Here's what was discussed:

1. The meeting was called open at 20:00 by James Johns.
2. A vote was taken to name the club "Bristol Riichi Mahjong". The vote passed.
3. The proposed constitution was discussed. An amendment was suggested to increase the number of members required to request an AGM from 2 to 4. A vote was taken to adopt the amended constitution. The vote passed.
4. Tom Pearson was elected Chair.
5. James Johns was elected Co-chair.
6. A vote was taken to adopt the club logo as seen on the website. The vote passed.
7. A vote was taken for the club to seek affiliation with the UK Mahjong Association. The vote passed.
8. The method in which inaugural members would be determined was explained. No objections were raised. The method was as follows: everyone who filled in the signup form which had been distributed on Discord and Facebook, and has also done one of the following:
   1. attended this meeting
   1. played at least one hand of riichi at one of our meetups in the last year
9. The meeting was closed at 20:46.
